About Free Students Union

The Free Student Union (FSU) is a student representative body found in many government colleges in Nepal. It is formed through direct elections, where elected candidates serve for a two-year term.

Our mission is to ensure that every student's voice is heard and their rights are protected. We work tirelessly to create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and personally.

The FSU organizes various events throughout the year, including cultural programs, academic seminars, and sports competitions. We also provide support services for students who need academic or personal assistance.
