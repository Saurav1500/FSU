About Us

ABOUT FREE STUDENT UNION(FSU)

The Free Student Union (FSU) is a student representative body found in many government colleges in Nepal. It is formed through direct elections, where elected candidates serve for a two-year term. The union plays a crucial role in advocating for student rights, organizing co-curricular activities, and acting as a bridge between students and administration
